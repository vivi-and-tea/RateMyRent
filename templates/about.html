About

This website is a project made for fun by Valerie Yamanaka for her portfolio. It was created in Atom using Python and Flask, using Bulma for frontend support and basing it's design on this Bulma template, with original art.

It is for entertainment purposes only and not meant to be seriously used to choose where to live.

All average rents used are from Suumo's data, aggregated from their own listings. They do not claim accuracy for this data.

Rating



Ratings are given based on the percentage the average rent would by the estimated monthly salary, assuming around 30% as the gold standard (S rank), and over 60 as being a fail (F) rating.

Recommendations



Recommendations are also based on the salary to rent percentage, and compares all rents across the city to find the lowest rent that is about 25% but below 50% of the estimated monthly salary.
